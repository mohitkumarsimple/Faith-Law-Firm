Legal Guide

Registering an LLP in India

A complete step-by-step guide to registering a Limited Liability Partnership for a Law Firm.

Step 01 & 02

Planning & Digital Signature

Choose the LLP name, partners (minimum 2), designated partners, and office address. Ensure compliance with Bar Council rules. Each designated partner needs a Class 3 DSC, PAN, Aadhaar, photo, email, and mobile.

Step 03 & 04

Name Reservation & Incorporation

Search the MCA database to avoid identical names and check trademark availability. Prepare the FILLIP form with partner details, office proof, NOC, business object, and DIN allotment if needed.

Step 05 & 06

Documents & LLP Agreement

Gather PAN, Aadhaar, address proof, photo, and rent agreement/NOC. Execute the LLP Agreement within 30 days of incorporation, covering capital, profit ratio, dispute resolution, and dissolution rules.

Step 07 & 08

MCA Filing & PAN/TAN/Bank

File Form 3 within 30 days after incorporation. Obtain PAN/TAN, open a bank account using the COI, PAN, LLP Agreement, and KYC. Register for GST if applicable.

Annual Compliance & Practical Tips

Maintain proper books of accounts and file Form 11 (Annual Return), Form 8 (Statement of Accounts), and Income Tax Return annually. Comply with TDS/GST obligations throughout the year. Keep digital records, use professional email addresses, draft clear engagement letters, maintain strict client confidentiality, and consider obtaining professional indemnity insurance to safeguard the firm and its clients.
